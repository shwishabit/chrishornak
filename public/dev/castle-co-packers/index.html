The Castle difference

Brewed from real tea leaves. Never from powder.

Most bottled tea starts as a powder or concentrate — faster, cheaper, and you can taste it. Castle is one of a handful of contract producers in the country that brews bottled tea straight from the leaf, the way the best brands insist on. When the product is the whole pitch, the bottling can't be the weak link.

  • Real-leaf brewingA cleaner, truer flavor than powder or concentrate.
  • High-end focusBuilt around specialized brands, not commodity runs.
  • Recipe fidelityYour formula, bottled the way you intended it.
