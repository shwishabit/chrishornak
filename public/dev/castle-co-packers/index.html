The Castle difference

Brewed from real tea leaves. Never from powder.

Most bottled tea starts as a powder or concentrate — faster, cheaper, and you can taste it. Castle is one of a handful of contract producers in the country that brews bottled tea directly from the leaf, the way the best brands insist on.

It's the same reason high-end, specialized brands bring their recipes here: when the product is the whole pitch, the bottling can't be the weak link.

  • Real-leaf brewingA cleaner, truer flavor than powder or concentrate.
  • High-end focusBuilt around specialized brands, not commodity runs.
  • Recipe fidelityYour formula, bottled the way you intended it.

A beverage family, four generations deep

President & CEO Brian Dworkin came up in a Connecticut beverage family whose roots run back to 1904. He took over the former Chestnut Ridge Beverage plant in Latrobe and rebuilt it line by line — from a single production line into a five-line, state-of-the-art bottling operation that recycles 90% of its materials.
