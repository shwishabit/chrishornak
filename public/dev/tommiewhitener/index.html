about the author
Tommie W. Whitener

A former journalist and trial lawyer who came to fiction in his sixties. Over a forty-five‑year legal career split between criminal defense and family law, he learned to watch people closely — the worst at their best, the best at their worst. That observation now fuels a body of work ranging from rural Arkansas (Tom & Leone) to nineteenth‑century Russia (The Circassian Beauty) to contemporary Hollywood (The Showrunner). He writes from Novato, California.
