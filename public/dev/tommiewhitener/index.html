about me
Tommie W. Whitener

After previous careers in journalism and law, I came to fiction late but with passion. Calling on my world experiences in California and from Dubai to Russia for inspiration and with a lifetime of enthusiastic reading as background, my writings feature people as they really are, by turns loving and loved, idealistic and endearing, and, unfortunately, at the same time, deeply flawed.
